## HATUA — Terms of Service
Last Updated: March 28, 2026
1. The Service
HATUA ("the App") is an open-source fitness tracking platform. By using the App, you agree to these terms. If you do not agree, you must stop using the App and delete your account.
2. The Points Economy ("Hatua Points")
 * Accrual: Points are earned at a rate of 1 point per 1 Kilometre (1,000 metres).
 * Welcome Bonus: New users receive 20 points upon successful registration and first login.
 * Referrals: Both the referrer and the referee receive 20 points once the referee completes their first tracked kilometre.
 * No Monetary Value: Hatua Points are NOT a currency or "e-money" under the Central Bank of Kenya regulations until the moment of redemption. They cannot be transferred between users.
3. M-Pesa Redemptions
 * Conversion: Points are redeemed at a rate of 100 Points = KSH 10.00.
 * Minimum Threshold: A minimum of 500 Points (KSH 50.00) is required for any M-Pesa cash-out.
 * Verification: HATUA reserves the right to delay or deny redemptions if "GPS Spoofing" or fraudulent activity (e.g., placing the phone in a vehicle to mimic walking) is detected.
 * Fees: Any transaction fees charged by Safaricom (M-Pesa) during the B2C payout will be deducted from the final payout amount.
4. Subscription Tiers (KSH)
 * Msingi (Basic): KSH 100/year.
 * Nguvu (Pro): KSH 500/year.
 * Dhahabu (Platinum): KSH 1,000/year.
 * Subscriptions are non-refundable once the premium features have been accessed.
5. Privacy & Identity
 * Users are encouraged to use the Avatar System (Simba, Chui, etc.) to maintain privacy on public leaderboards.
 * Your GPS data is used solely for distance calculation and route suggestions. We do not sell your location history to third parties, in compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019).

HATUA is a tracking tool, not a medical advisor. Consult a healthcare professional before starting any new or intense physical activity. HATUA is not liable for injuries sustained while using the App.
7. Termination
We reserve the right to ban any account found to be manipulating GPS data or abusing the referral system. Banned accounts forfeit all accrued points.
