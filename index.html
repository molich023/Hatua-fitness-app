What HATUA Offers

Everything you need to crush your goals

Built specifically for Kenya — AI-powered, weather-aware, and M-Pesa native. No other fitness app comes close.

🧠
AI Coach & Route Learning
Our built-in AI learns your routes, average distances, and peak performance times. It adapts recommendations to your unique patterns — like a personal coach in your pocket.
🌦
AI Weather Forecasting
Hyperlocal 7-day forecasts with AI prediction engine. Rain alerts, optimal running windows, UV index warnings, and weather-adaptive route suggestions for Nairobi and beyond.
🗺
Topology-Aware Routes
AI scans local terrain topology to discover new routes you haven't explored. Elevation maps, safety scores, and POI markers for Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu and more.
📊
Advanced Visualisations
Beautiful real-time charts: distance trends, calorie burn, pace progression, activity breakdown, and weather-fitness correlation. All rendered live in the app.
⭐
Hatua Points Economy
Earn points for every step, streak, and referral. Redeem for M-Pesa cash, airtime, gym passes, Nike vouchers, marathon entries, or even plant a tree in Karura!
💚
M-Pesa Native Payments
Pay via Lipa Na M-Pesa Paybill 522522. Instant STK Push to your phone. No credit card, no bank account needed. 100% Kenya-native billing.
🔴
Live Route Progress
Track your position in real-time along any route. See distance done, km remaining, ETA to finish, and live speed — all updating every second during your session.
🏆
Community Leaderboard
Compete on Weekly, Monthly, and All-Time leaderboards. City-level rankings for Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu. Privacy-first — avatar-only, never real names.
🔒
OWASP Top 10 Security
All 10 OWASP security standards implemented: JWT tokens, Bcrypt hashing, AES-256 GPS encryption, rate limiting, OTP verification, and Kenya DPA 2019 compliance.
💚
Pay with M-Pesa
No credit card. No bank account. Just your Safaricom M-Pesa. Instant STK Push sent to your phone — enter your PIN and you're subscribed.
